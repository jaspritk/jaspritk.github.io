Who we are?

Kaurative Resin Art is a two person small business based in Mehsana, Gujarat. We are specialised in producing custom-made items out of high-quality epoxy resin that will outlast your memory, such as photo frames, keychains, pendants, bracelets, card holders, car hangings, polaroid frames, fridge magnets, rakhis, t-light holders and many more.

Let's take a sneak peek to our Awesomeeee Journey!!! > Me and my cousin stared this business as we have keen interest in nature. As memory collectors, we have observed that while individuals desire to maintain things, they often lack the proper starting point. This is where resin art comes into play, as it is not only robust but also fully customizable for people who want to add a touch of their individuality and display creativity. O:)

We began learning resin art in April 2023 and are experimenting with various resins and moulds to see which works best. At first, we wasted a lot of goods and moulds due to improper hardener and epoxy resin mixing. It's a challenging start. We continued, even so, concentrating more on the combinations and arrangements of the components. And after a while, everything began to make sense.

Regarding our customised packaging, we designed our visitor cards, thank-you cards (each present is accompanied by a handwritten note expressing our gratitude for your assistance), and stickers and had them professionally printed by Vista Print India.
